Prospectus register

This register allows users to search for and download prospectuses, registration documents, universal registration documents, securities notes, summaries, supplements, amendments and final terms that have been approved by national competent authorities in the European Economic Area. Users can search for documents using various criteria, such as an issuer's name (by entering their full name in the search field or by entering a part of their name ) or LEI, the ISIN number of the securities, the Members States in which the securities may be offered and/or listed on a regulated market and the type of the securities.

Where a prospectus is made up of separate documents, such as a registration document, securities note and summary or a base prospectus with final terms, the prospectus register links the various documents comprising the prospectus. The prospectus register also displays the supplements that have amended the information in a prospectus.

Registration documents and universal registration documents are only included in the register if they have been used in a prospectus. If the registration document or universal registration document is not (yet) available in ESMA's prospectus register, users may be interested in searching for it on the issuer's website.

ESMA has compiled this register on the basis of notifications from national competent authorities in accordance with Article 25 of Regulation 2017/1129 (the 'Prospectus Regulation').

Disclaimer

ESMA does not provide any representation or warranty that the available content on this website are complete, accurate or up to date. The mere fact that a prospectus has been approved and published implies nothing about the probity or creditworthiness of the issuer.

Responsibility for prospectuses and any related documentation made available through this register lies with the issuer or the person responsible for drawing up the prospectus and of competent authorities, to the extent that the latter approve prospectuses on the basis of a scrutiny of the completeness, consistency and comprehensibility of the information given in the prospectus and are required to upload any documentation in accordance with the Prospectus Regulation. Questions regarding the content of prospectus should be directed to the national competent authority responsible for the approval of the prospectus or the issuer/person responsible for drawing up the prospectus.

The making of prospectuses available through this register does not constitute publication within the meaning of art. 3 (1) and 21 of the Prospectus Regulation. It therefore does not release those concerned from any relevant legal obligation.
